Welcome to We Do Care Foundation

Nep Care Foundation is a non-governmental, non-profit, independent public foundation. For more than a month, Nep Care Foundation has developed and coordinated various programs to improve the conditions of those in need, such as the elderly, orphans and displaced people.

Our fundamental mission is to empower the needy people through education and assistance to live a dignified life and reach their aspirations by changing themselves their social, cultural and economical backgrounds.

We also support your fundraising with Local Support Services. These include everything from a Local Representative to Fundraising Support such as Grant Research and Writing and Event Management

Let's put our hands together and make efforts in making the lives of orphanage and elderly people better through Nep CARE FOUNDATION. Let's show our love, affection and care for them. If we can reduce the sufferings and make the world much better place, why not?

Our Objectives And Goals

The main objective of Nep Care Foundation is to support, help and protect those who are needy and to solve their specific problems. The Foundation stands for tolerance, equal opportunity and good life conditions for everyone. It works for different non-profit organization of Nepal.

For the achievement of our goals, Help the Needy organizes and participates in forums, seminars, conferences and other meetings on national and international levels and it cooperates and exchanges information with organizations, institutions and individuals in Nepal and abroad.

It is indeed an Foundation of " people with good hearts " .
